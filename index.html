How to Play
  • No sound, ever: The mouther sees one short clue — just one or two words — and mouths it to their team completely silently. No voice, no whisper, nothing.
  • Team reads the lips: Everyone on the mouther's team watches their mouth and shouts guesses. The mouther taps Got it! the instant they're right, or Skip to move on — no penalties, ever.
  • Score: One-word clues are worth +1, two-word clues are worth +2. Skips score nothing but cost nothing either.
  • Time's up: Whatever's on screen when the buzzer hits is up for review next screen — you can fix a missed tap before it locks in.
  • Most points when every team has had their turns wins!
